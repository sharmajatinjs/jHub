The Dancer A.K.A
CHA HAE-IN (차해인)

Cha Hae-In is the Vice-Guild Master of the Hunters Guild and the sword woman of the main raiding party. She is the 9th ranked Hunter in South Korea also being its only female S-Rank Hunter. Hae-In was a direct participant of the 4th Jeju Island Reclamation Raid as a member of the Korean S-Rank Reckoner team. She possesses the unique ability of mana detection, which allows her to sense the presence of mana and distinguish it from other hunters except for Sung Jin-Woo.

S RANK
Shadow Monarch A.K.A
SUNG JIN WOO (성진우)

Sung Jin-Woo is the protagonist of Solo Leveling. He was previously known as the "Weakest Hunter of All Mankind" due to his low rank and feeble strength. However, after the path of becoming the World's Strongest Hunter, he was Reawakened and became a Player after surviving the Cartenon Temple and completing the secret quest "Courage of the Weak." The mysterious System has allowed Jin-Woo to "level up" and increases his abilities and strength until he is reawakened.
